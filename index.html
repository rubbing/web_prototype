OUR DECLARATION

Climate Council aims to inform and allow people to sign up and support their plans in tackling the climate crisis. Our main goals are to build momentum from the ground up, by working with journalists, businesses, community leaders, and people to drive change.

We aim to teach people:

      1. What climate change is.
      2. The importance of tackling it.
      3. And how they are going to do it.
